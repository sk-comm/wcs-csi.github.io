SINGTEL CYBER SECURITY INSTITUTE

REAL-WORLD ENVIRONMENT

The Range provides both real and virtual environments for you to simulate your organisation's environment and infrastructure, to conduct heavy testing in a safe and controlled setting.

TESTING

Combining the lastest threat intelligence and attack tools, we are able to create up-to-date attack scenarios targeted at your enterprise's potential areas of vulnerability.

TRAINING

We adopt a holistic enterprise-wide view towards cyber resiliency, conducting programmes targeted from the technical levels to strategic management levels.
